Method

1. Preheat oven to 180C°/350°F (160°C fan). 2.Grease 2 x 22cm/9" cake pans with butter, then line the base. 3.Sift flour, cocoa, baking powder and baking soda into a large bowl. Add Sugar and salt. Whisk briefly to combine. 4.Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Whisk well to combine until lump free - about 30 seconds. 5.Add boiling water and whisk to incorporate. The batter is VERY thin (see video). 6.Pour batter into cake pans. 7.Bake for 35 minutes or until a wooden skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. 8. Cool for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks upside down. 9.Cool completely before frosting. I frosted the cake with my Chocolate Buttercream Frosting.
